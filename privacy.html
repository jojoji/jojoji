Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 16, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Hong Kong Pupu Network Technology Co., Limited (doing business as "Pupu Games", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our mobile games ("Games") and related services.

By downloading and playing our Games, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Most of the information we collect is limited to "Device Information" and "Gameplay Data" which does not directly identify you as a named individual.

A. Information Collected Automatically

When you use our Games, we may automatically collect the following information through our third-party partners (such as advertising networks and analytics providers):

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, screen resolution, language settings, timezone, and mobile carrier.
  • Identifiers: Advertising IDs (Google ADID, Apple IDFA) and unique device identifiers.
  • Network Information: IP address (which may be used to estimate your general location, e.g., country or city).
  • Gameplay Data: Game progress, scores, achievements, and interactions within the game.

B. Information You Provide

We do not require you to create an account to play our Games. However, we may collect information if you contact us directly for support (e.g., your email address and the content of your message).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the Game functionality.
  • To analyze game usage and optimize player experience (e.g., fixing crashes or difficulty balancing).
  • To serve personalized advertisements (via third-party ad networks).
  • To prevent fraud and ensure security.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. Third-Party Services and Advertising

Since our Games are free to play, we work with third-party advertising partners to serve ads. These partners may collect and use your data (such as Advertising IDs and IP addresses) to provide personalized ads.

We may share data with the following types of third-party service providers:

  • Ad Networks: Including but not limited to AppLovin, Pangle (TikTok), Google AdMob, ironSource, Unity Ads, and Meta Audience Network.
  • Analytics Providers: Such as Google Firebase, GameAnalytics, or AppsFlyer.

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third-party service providers directly.

4. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have specific rights regarding your data:

  • Opt-out of Targeted Ads: You can opt-out of interest-based advertising by adjusting the settings on your device (e.g., "Opt out of Ads Personalization" on Android or "Limit Ad Tracking" on iOS).
  • Access and Deletion: You may request access to or deletion of the personal data we hold about you by contacting us. Note that since we do not store user accounts, we may identify you only by your Device ID.
  • GDPR (EEA/UK Users): You have the right to access, rectify, or erase your personal data.
  • CCPA (California Users): You have the right to opt-out of the sale of your personal information.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy. Gameplay data and analytics are generally retained for a limited period to assist in game improvements.

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to help protect information about you from loss, theft, misuse, and unauthorized access. However, no transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Children's Privacy

Our Games are not directed to children under the age of 13 (or other age thresholds required by local laws). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn that we have collected personal information of a child, we will take steps to delete such information from our files as soon as possible.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to, and processed in, countries other than the country in which you are resident (such as servers in the United States or Singapore). These countries may have data protection laws that are different from the laws of your country.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
